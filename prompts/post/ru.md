## Platform-Specific Requirements

### For "vk" Platform (VKontakte):
- **Character Count**: 700-900 characters (optimal for VK engagement)
- **Hashtags**: 3-7 hashtags naturally integrated at the end
- **Structure**:
  - Opening (first 2 lines): Hook the reader, create curiosity
  - Body: Develop message with conversational tone, specific details
  - Closing: Natural conclusion or call-to-action
- **Style**: Authentic, relatable to Russian-speaking audience, personal anecdotes welcome

### For "facebook" Platform:
- **Character Count**: 40-80 characters (short and impactful)
- **Hashtags**: Minimal (0-2 hashtags)
- **Structure**:
  - Single powerful statement or question
  - Warm, welcoming tone
- **Style**: Friendly, inclusive, family-oriented content

### For "dzen" Platform (Yandex Dzen):
- **Character Count**: 2000-2500 characters (article-style format)
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 hashtags at the end
- **Structure**:
  - Compelling introduction with hook
  - Body with subheadings (use **Bold** for headings)
  - Practical, actionable information
  - Clear sections with paragraphs
  - Summary or call-to-action
- **Style**: Educational, structured, authoritative but accessible

### enthusiastic

### informative

- Clear explanations
- Value-driven content
- Fact-based approach

## Technical Requirements

### Character Limits (STRICT):
- **vk**: 700-900 characters including hashtags
- **facebook**: 40-80 characters
- **dzen**: 2000-2500 characters including hashtags

### Hashtag Guidelines:
- **vk**: 3-7 hashtags (e.g., #мотивация #lifestyle #успех)
- **facebook**: 0-2 hashtags
- **dzen**: 3-5 hashtags

### Style Guidelines:
✅ **DO:**
- Write in {{language}} exclusively
- Match platform's character limit EXACTLY
- Use emojis sparingly and naturally
- Create specific, concrete examples
- Vary sentence length
- Build emotional connection

❌ **DON'T:**
- Exceed or fall short of character limits
- Use clickbait or misleading hooks
- Overuse hashtags
- Write in foreign languages
- Use overly formal language (unless professional tone)
- Include external links in body text (platforms often suppress them)

## Output Format

1. Write the complete post in {{language}}

3. Include hashtags at the end (per platform requirements)
4. Character count must match platform specifications
5. NO "Here's the post:" or similar prefixes
6. For dzen: use **Bold** for subheadings

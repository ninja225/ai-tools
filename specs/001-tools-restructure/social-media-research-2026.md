## Cross-Platform Principles (All Audiences)

### Universal Best Practices
1. **Mobile-first**: 80%+ read on phones - short paragraphs essential
2. **Value-first**: Every post must answer "So what?"
3. **Authenticity**: 2026 audiences detect and reject fake
4. **Engagement design**: Every post should invite specific action
5. **Visual quality**: Even text posts benefit from clean formatting
6. **Consistency**: Regular posting > sporadic brilliance
7. **Community**: Respond to comments, build relationships
8. **Testing**: A/B test times, formats, hooks

### Language-Specific Considerations
- **Russian**: Direct, substantial, skeptical - prove value
- **English**: Inclusive, story-driven, emotionally resonant
- **Arabic**: Respectful, family-focused, culturally sensitive

### 2026 Algorithm Reality
- **Authenticity signals**: Platforms detect and boost genuine engagement
- **Dwell time**: How long users engage matters more than likes
- **Completion**: Finishing reading/watching is key metric
- **Shares > likes**: Distribution indicates value
- **Comment quality**: Substantive discussion > emoji spam
- **Save/bookmark**: Strongest signal of value
